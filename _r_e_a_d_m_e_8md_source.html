1 # Helium for Arduino #
2 
3 ![Build Status](https://travis-ci.org/helium/helium-arduino.svg?branch=master)
4 
5 This code repository exposes an [Arduino](https://www.arduino.cc/)
6 library for
7 the [Helium Atom](https://www.helium.com/products/atom-xbee-module)
8 module. The Helium Atom makes it easy to securely connect IoT devices
9 and applications to back-end IoT services.
